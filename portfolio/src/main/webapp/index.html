About Me

Who I Am?

Hey, my name is Joshua Lewis. I am 19 years old and was born and raised in a small town called Saint Rose, Louisiana. I completed the majority of my schooling in Louisiana, but in my senior year of high school, I moved to Heath, Texas. I think this move fits my personality as I love meeting new people and making new memories!

What I Do?

I am currently a rising sophomore Computer Science major at Xavier University of Louisiana and am expected to graduate in May of 2023! I am a software mentor for a local FRC (Robotics) Team in my community as well as a tutor for some of my peers at Xavier. I also am currently working a Google this summer as a STEP intern which is one of the things I am most proud of!

Why I do it?

I got into computer science because of Robotics during my freshman year of high-school and from that point on I've been obsessed with it. Seeing the code that I'm writing affecting a robot was something that never got old. My goal as I get further into my career is to work in the robotics health industry or the autonomous vehicle industry. This is because those are areas where a lot of people can be positively affected as well as an area that I truly enjoy working in. Of course with the future hopes of owning my own company!
